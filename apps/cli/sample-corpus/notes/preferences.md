# Preferences (so I don't keep re-explaining myself)

- **Coffee:** flat white, oat milk, no sugar. Decaf after 2pm.
- **Writing:** terse. Lead with the answer, then the reason. No preamble, no
  "I hope this helps".
- **Meetings:** mornings only if possible; I think worst after 4pm.
- **Notifications:** batch them. One digest beats five pings. Quiet after 9pm.
- **Travel:** aisle seat, carry-on only, no early-morning flights.
