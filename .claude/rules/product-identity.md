# Product identity

- Muse is a continuing personal AI for one person's daily life and work. Never reduce the
  whole product to a work assistant, productivity tracker, or computer-use agent.
- Attunement means learning how to fit help into that person's life. Personal Continuity is
  the first roadmap proof: restore a user-chosen unfinished life or work thread, learn from
  the outcome, and improve the next help.
- Muse Work and Work Resumption are work-specific modes under Personal Continuity, not the
  product boundary.
- Observe is optional evidence for better timing. It must not become the product identity or
  a prerequisite for the user-invoked Continuity Pack.
